\section{Related Work}

Causal convolutions, state-space sequence models, recurrent attention alternatives, conditional computation, and lexical memory mechanisms all motivate parts of this study. We do not claim novelty for depthwise causal convolution, deterministic expert assignment, shared expert computation, or token-indexed embeddings individually. The question studied here is whether their constrained integration provides a useful quality--throughput--state tradeoff at small language-model scale.
